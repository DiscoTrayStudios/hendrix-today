Hendrix Today App

Stay up to date with events at Hendrix College

We're working hard to finish our development, stay tuned!

Welcome to the new Hendrix Today app. Here you can find all the new and upcoming events happening at Hendrix College.

Click on an event to see the whole description. You can even share events with friends or add it to your phone's calendar.

Use the calendar page to look ahead and see what's happeening that month.

The Resource's Page has quick options that can be used to submit your own event to Hendrix Today, reserving a room on campus, viewing the daily Cafeteria menu, contacting Public Safety, or viewing a map of the campus.
